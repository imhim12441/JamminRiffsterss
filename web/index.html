About Us

We’re a group of high schoolers making music that’s loud, raw, and full of energy. With a mix of rock and pop influences, we create songs that hit hard and stick with you. Every track is a blend of powerful riffs, catchy melodies, and the kind of passion that keeps us coming back for more. Whether on stage or in the studio, we’re all about making music that moves people.

Stay tuned, and let’s turn up the volume!
